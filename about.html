My story

My name is Luiza Moshkin –

I am originally from Kyrgyzstan, a small country, surrounded by mountains, located in Central Asia. There, I worked as both a graphic designer and a language teacher. However, in 2017, I decided to move to Canada to pursue my career in education. I began teaching languages and also worked on obtaining my Doctoral degree from the University of Toronto. However, during the pandemic, I took the time to reconsider my career path and realized that I wanted to return to the world of graphic design. It was then that I discovered coding and became a web developer. Now, I live in Luxembourg city. I truly believe that web development is the perfect combination of my skills in design, communication, and problem-solving. I find great pleasure in creating websites that are both visually appealing and fully responsive. My constant desire to learn and improve has helped me stay ahead of the curve and provide my clients with the best possible solutions to their needs.
