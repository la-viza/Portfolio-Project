About Me

I'm Luiza Moshkin –

A front-end developer from Toronto, Canada

I am originally from Kyrgyzstan, Central Asia, where I worked as a graphic designer and a language teacher. In 2017, I moved to Canada and started my carrer in education, teaching languages and working on my Doctoral degree in University of Toronto. During the time of pandemic, I reconsidered my carrer choices and decided to return to the graphic design world. And that is when I discovered coding. I became a web-developer because I truly believe that this area combines all my skills in design, my communication skills and my passion to solve challenging tasks. I immensively enjoy to create aesthetically pleasing websites that are fully responsive. My constant urge to learn new things always pushes me to move forward and makes me broaden my knowledge to meet the needs of my clients. See my projects
